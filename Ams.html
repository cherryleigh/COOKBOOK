Midnight Snack

Ingredients

  1. All-purpose flour
  2. Sugar
  3. Baking powder
  4. Salt
  5. Milk
  6. Eggs
  7. Butter or oil (melted)
  8. Vanilla extract (optional)
  9. Chocolate syrup

Steps

  1. Preheat the waffle maker
  2. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt
  3. Add milk, eggs, melted butter, and vanilla
  4. Mix until smooth batter forms
  5. Lightly grease the waffle maker
  6. Pour batter into the waffle maker
  7. Cook until waffles are golden brown
  8. Remove waffles and place on a plate
  9. Drizzle chocolate syrup on top
  10. Serve warm
