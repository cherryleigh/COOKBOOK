Dinner

Ingredients

  1. Meat (chicken, beef, or pork)
  2. Onion
  3. Potatoes
  4. Carrots
  5. Japanese curry roux blocks
  6. Water
  7. Cooking oil
  8. Salt (optional)

Steps

  1. Cut meat and vegetables into bite-sized pieces
  2. Heat oil in a pot
  3. Saut√© onions until soft
  4. Add meat and cook until lightly browned
  5. Add potatoes and carrots
  6. Pour in water and bring to a boil
  7. Reduce heat and simmer until ingredients are tender
  8. Turn off heat and add curry roux blocks
  9. Stir until roux is fully dissolved
  10. Simmer again until curry thickens
  11. Serve hot with rice
