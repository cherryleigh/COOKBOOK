Lunch

Ingredients

  1. Chicken fillet
  2. Salt
  3. Black pepper
  4. Garlic
  5. Soy sauce
  6. Cooking oil
  7. Flour or breadcrumbs
  8. Egg

Steps

  1. Season the chicken fillet with salt, pepper, and garlic
  2. Prepare flour or breadcrumbs in a bowl
  3. Beat the egg in a separate bowl
  4. Dip the chicken into the egg
  5. Coat the chicken with flour or breadcrumbs
  6. Heat oil in a pan
  7. Fry the chicken fillet until golden brown
  8. Cook until fully done inside
  9. Drain excess oil and serve
