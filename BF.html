Breakfast

Ingredients

  1. Cooked rice (preferably day-old)
  2. Cooking oil
  3. Eggs
  4. Garlic
  5. Onion or green onions
  6. Vegetables (carrots, peas, corn, bell peppers, etc.)
  7. Protein (chicken, shrimp, pork, beef, tofu â€“ optional)
  8. Soy sauce
  9. Salt
  10. Pepper
  11. Sesame oil (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan or wok
  2. Add garlic and onion, cook until fragrant
  3. Add protein and cook until done
  4. Add vegetables and stir-fry briefly
  5. Push ingredients to the side and scramble eggs
  6. Add cooked rice and mix well
  7. Add soy sauce, salt, and pepper
  8. Stir-fry until everything is evenly combined
  9. Finish with sesame oil and green onions
